## What Is Floux

Floux is a WhatsApp AI agent that recovers clients lost to missed calls at beauty salons in Madrid.

When a client calls a salon and nobody picks up, Floux automatically sends a WhatsApp from the salon's number, holds a natural-language conversation, and books the appointment directly into Booksy, Treatwell, or Google Calendar.

**One-line positioning:** "Recuperamos los clientes que pierdes cuando no puedes coger el teléfono."

**English equivalent:** "We recover the clients you lose every time you can't pick up the phone."

---

## The Problem We Solve

Beauty salon owners in Madrid are solo operators or small teams. While they are cutting hair or doing nails, the phone rings — and they can't answer. That missed call is a missed booking.

- 62% of clients who don't reach a salon call a competitor immediately
- 85% of those clients never call back
- A regular client is worth ~€1,750/year in LTV
- The average salon misses 5–8 calls per week
- Salons with appointment reminders see 30–40% fewer no-shows

The owner is both the service provider and the receptionist. They cannot do both at once.

- Integrations: Booksy, Treatwell, Google Calendar

## Target Customer (ICP)

**Primary:** Beauty salon owner, west Madrid, 1–8 staff, owner-operated

### Demographics
- Age: 45–60 years old
- Gender: predominantly female (peluquerías mujer, centros de estética)
- Tech comfort: low-to-medium — uses WhatsApp daily, may use Instagram, hesitant with new apps
- Income: middle class, self-employed (autónoma)
- Location priority: Chamberí, Argüelles, Moncloa, Carabanchel, Latina, Majadahonda, Pozuelo de Alarcón

### Psychographics
- Proud of their work and their client relationships
- Overwhelmed by admin tasks that take time away from actual service
- Skeptical of technology they don't understand
- Trusts word-of-mouth and local reputation above all
- Does NOT want to feel replaced — wants to feel supported

### Current Behavior
- Books 40–70% of appointments via WhatsApp (manual)
- Uses Booksy or Treatwell for 20–30% (but often ignores platform features)
- Receives 5–8 missed calls per week during service hours
- Responds to WhatsApp messages at night or between clients
- Does not use CRM, loyalty programs, or client reactivation campaigns

### Salon Types
- Peluquería mujer (haircut, color, treatments) — largest segment
- Centro de estética (nails, facial, waxing, massage)
- Peluquería mixta
- Barbería (fastest growing, younger owners, more tech-comfortable)

## Positioning

**Category:** AI booking agent for beauty salons
**Frame:** Missed-call recovery tool, NOT "AI receptionist" (triggers defensive reaction)
**Vs. Booksy/Treatwell:** Complements them — Floux captures the clients who never reach the platform
**Vs. WhatsApp Business auto-replies:** Floux holds a real conversation and actually books; auto-replies just send a message

### What We Are NOT
- Not a CRM
- Not a booking platform
- Not a receptionist replacement
- Not "inteligencia artificial" (never use this term with owners)

### Competitive Landscape
- **Booksy** — most adopted platform; owners complain about commissions and complexity
- **Treatwell** — marketplace model; takes high commission; salon depends on Treatwell for traffic
- **Fresha** — free software but charges % on payments; growing in Madrid
- **WhatsApp Business** — widely used; owners don't know advanced features; no AI
- **No direct AI competitor** in west Madrid yet (first-mover advantage)

## Sales Context

### Go-to-Market
- **Channel:** In-person door-to-door in west Madrid salons
- **Pitch:** Show the product, don't explain it — live demo of a simulated missed-call recovery
- **Sales cycle:** 1–3 visits; decision maker is the owner (present in the salon)
- **Start date:** Monday 2026-04-13

### Sales Approach
- Lead with the pain ("cuántas llamadas pierdes esta semana?")
- Anchor on one recovered client covering the monthly cost
- Avoid: technical explanations, AI terminology, comparisons to receptionists
- Use: specific numbers from Gemini research, ROI calculator on the spot

### Top 3 Objections + Rebuttals

| Objection | Rebuttal |
|-----------|----------|
| "Ya tengo Booksy/Treatwell" | "Perfecto — Floux captura a los que llaman antes de llegar a Booksy. Son clientes distintos." |
| "Es muy caro, €97 al mes" | "Un cliente recuperado al mes lo paga. ¿Cuántas llamadas pierdes esta semana?" |
| "No me fío de que un robot hable con mis clientes" | "No es un robot — es como si alguien del equipo respondiera al WhatsApp. ¿Te enseño?" |
