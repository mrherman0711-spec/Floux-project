# Brand Voice

## Positioning
"Recuperamos los clientes que pierdes cuando no puedes coger el teléfono."
NOT: "AI receptionist", "booking platform", "Fresha competitor"

## Tone
- Direct, practical, no tech jargon
- Warm but not cute — these are busy business owners
- Results-first: always lead with what they gain (recovered clients, €€€)

## Language Rules
- NEVER say: "inteligencia artificial", "IA", "bot", "robot", "automático" to customers
- NEVER compare Floux to a receptionist
- DO say: "sistema", "asistente", "recuperamos"
- Use tú with clients, never usted

## Target Audience (priority order)
1. Centros de estética/belleza, west Madrid, 2-8 staff, owner-operated
2. Inmobiliarias (after first case studies)
3. Clínicas privadas (after salon + real estate track record)

## Pricing Copy
- Básico: €47/mes — WhatsApp automático sin conversación IA
- Agente: €97/mes + €197 setup — agente completo (core product)
- Completo: €147/mes + €297 setup — agente + reseñas + recordatorios
- Anchor: "Se paga solo con UN cliente recuperado al mes"
